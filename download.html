The steps for downloading the log files are as follows -

  • Open Mozilla Firefox and in the upper right corner of the screen click on the red icon of the web-extension
  • Now, click once on the option 'Log data 1' and then click on 'Download Console Log data'. Save the txt file.
  • Now, click once on the option 'Log data 2' and then click on 'Download Console Log data'. Save the txt file.
  • Repeat the above steps six times. (2 clicks per step)
  • Finally save all the six txt files in a .zip folder and name it appropriately as mentioned.
