The log file must be named [Code]_[Age]_[Gender]_[City].pdf

Here [Code] is your (or of the IITK student who hired you) IITK roll number,[Age] is your age in number of years, [Gender] is M (Male), F (Female) or O (Other) and [City] is the name of your current city of residence. For instance, if my code is XYZ, am 21 years old, a female and reside in Delhi, then the name of my submitted file should be XYZ_21_F_Delhi.pdf
